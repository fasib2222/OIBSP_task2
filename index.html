Michelle Obama

First Lady of the United States

Michelle Obama is an American lawyer, author, and the wife of the 44th President of the United States, Barack Obama. She was the first African American First Lady of the United States, serving from 2009 to 2017.

Accomplishments

During her time as First Lady, Michelle Obama focused on issues such as healthy eating, education, and support for military families. She launched the Let's Move! campaign to combat childhood obesity and promoted healthy eating habits. She also launched the Reach Higher initiative to encourage young people to pursue higher education.
